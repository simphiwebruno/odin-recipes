Banana Berry Milkshake

A Tasty Drink To Enjoy With Your Pizza

On a great summer day with your family or friends, a shake is drink you'd love to have with your pizza. The best part is that it only takes 5 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fresh strawberries
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • 1 cup of fresh peaches
  • 1 cup of apples
  • 1 1/2 cups of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 1/2 cups ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, banana slices, peaches, apples, and ice cream.
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add ice
  4. Pour in milk
  5. Blend again until smooth
  6. Serve immediately
